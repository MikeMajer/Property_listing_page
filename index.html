Property Descriprion

Boasting an ideal northerly aspect on a generous 625sqm allotment, 14 Sunny Avenue offers an enviable lifestyle for the largest of families. Designed to accommodate growing families and with quality of finish in mind, this stunning contemporary home will delight with spacious ground floor open plan living and provides a premium selection of outdoor entertaining options overseeing a sparkling in-ground pool.

The chef's kitchen, set at the heart of the home boasts new stone benchtops, double oven and breakfast bar, flows seamlessly out to one of two entertainment areas providing practical indoor-outdoor connectivity families and entertainers are sure to revel in. The pool house serves as a stunning second entertainment area, boasting outdoor cabinetry, stone bench tops, outdoor fridge and television.

A family-friendly floorplan sees a living area and four generous built-in bedrooms situated on the upper level, serviced by the main bathroom. The executive master suite boasts a generous walk-in robe and his-and-hers ensuite with double overhead rain shower heads.

Downstairs, an additional two living spaces plus generous study further exemplifies the practicality of the home and catering for those seeking separation.

The highest quality inclusions and finishes are evident throughout including:
  • Beautiful Bamboo flooring throughout
  • New luxurious pure wool carpet in bedrooms
  • Timber shutters and ceiling fans to all bedrooms
  • Laundry chute
  • Freshly painted interior and exterior
  • 60,000L inground salt water pool with solar heating
